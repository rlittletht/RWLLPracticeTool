Subscribing to iCalendar feed in Outlook 2016

You will need the internet address of the iCalendar feed (copy it from the practice tool site after clicking on "Get iCal Feed").

Adding an Internet Calendar

  • After booting Outlook, go to your Calendars by clicking on the Calendar icon in the lower left corner of your screen:

    • At this point you should see your calender(s) in the main window, and a list of calendars on the left side

  • Now right click on "Other Calendars", select Add Calender, and choose From Internet:
  • You will be prompted to enter the location of the internet calendar. This is where you paste in the link that you copied from the practice tool:

Once you click OK, and click OK again on the next dialog, you will be subscribed to the calendar you selected in the practice tool. If you ever want to remove this calendar in the future, just right click on the calendar (under Other Calendars) and choose Delete.
